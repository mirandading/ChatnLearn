What is ChatnLearn?

ChatnLearn is an innovative educational platform that uses AI-powered conversations to help students develop both subject knowledge and communication skills. Our interactive approach creates a safe space for learners to practice speaking, receive instant feedback, and build confidence through meaningful dialogue.

Why Oracy Matters

Oracy—the ability to express oneself fluently and grammatically in speech—is a fundamental skill that's often overlooked in traditional education.

Research shows that students who regularly engage in structured dialogue show improved critical thinking, deeper subject understanding, and greater confidence in academic settings.
