Why Oracy Matters

Oracy - the ability to articulate ideas through spoken language - is a fundamental skill that's often overlooked in traditional education.

Research shows that students who regularly engage in structured dialogue show improved critical thinking, deeper subject understanding, and greater confidence in academic settings.
